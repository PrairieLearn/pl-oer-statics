You are walking down the street in your hometown of DefinitelyARealPlace, USA when you come across your good friend officer Stickman. Officer Stickman remembers that your are a bright TAM 210/211 student, and decides to ask you what the reaction forces are at point a of the traffic light shown. The officer tells you that you may neglect the weight of the poles, all weights act in the negative y-direction, and that you are only looking for the reactions caused by the light fixtures. Ever the prepared student, you spring into action and calculate the reactions.
